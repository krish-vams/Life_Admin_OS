# Life Admin OS Project Context

## Project Overview

Life Admin OS is a full-stack personal productivity and automation platform.

The application helps users manage important personal responsibilities such as bills, subscriptions, documents, renewal dates, and reminders from one centralized dashboard.

Instead of depending on memory, scattered emails, notes, or calendars, users can track everything in one place. In advanced phases, the system can automatically detect subscriptions, bills, and payment reminders from Gmail and create reminders for the user.

## Problem Statement

Many people struggle to manage everyday responsibilities because important information is spread across different places.

Examples:

- Bills are received through email.
- Subscriptions renew automatically.
- Important documents expire without notice.
- Calendar reminders are often not created.
- People forget what services they are paying for monthly.

This can lead to missed payments, late fees, expired documents, and unnecessary spending.

## Proposed Solution

Life Admin OS provides a clean dashboard where users can manage and monitor important life-admin tasks.

The application allows users to:

- Track upcoming bills.
- Manage subscriptions.
- Store document expiry dates.
- Receive reminders before due dates.
- View monthly recurring expenses.
- Connect Gmail to detect subscriptions and bills automatically.
- Sync important dates with Google Calendar.

The goal is to reduce manual effort and help users stay organized.

## Core Product Pillars

- Visibility: show the user's upcoming responsibilities clearly.
- Prevention: surface due dates early enough to avoid missed payments and expirations.
- Cost awareness: make recurring spend visible.
- Low manual effort: reduce data entry over time through integrations.
- Trust: keep sensitive personal data clear, editable, and understandable.
